## NOTES FOR EDITOR

1. **Technical Terms:** Review for consistency in terminology (e.g., "dMRV" vs "digital MRV", "DLT" vs "blockchain")

2. **Capitalization:** Check consistency for terms like:
   - Web3 vs web3
   - Nature-based Solutions vs nature-based solutions
   - Digital Infrastructure vs digital infrastructure

3. **Numbers:** Review formatting consistency:
   - $2B+ vs $2 billion+
   - 10+ years vs 10 years+
   - 500M farmers vs 500 million farmers

4. **Industry Jargon:** Consider if terms need explanation:
   - ERPA (Emission Reduction Purchase Agreement)
   - REC (Renewable Energy Certificate)
   - RWA (Real World Assets)
   - dMRV (digital Measurement, Reporting & Verification)
   - DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology)

5. **Geographic References:** Ensure consistency:
   - APAC vs Asia Pacific
   - Frontier markets vs emerging markets

6. **Call-to-Action Consistency:** Review all CTAs for tone and urgency
